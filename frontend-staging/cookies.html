Cookie Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They help websites remember your preferences and session data.

2. Cookies We Use

CHIKA uses ONLY 1 cookie:

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
chika_demo_session Session tracking for rate limiting & chat history 30 days Essential (required for service)

3. Cookie Details

  • Name: chika_demo_session
  • Set by: chika.page backend
  • Contains: Random session ID (UUID format)
  • Security: HttpOnly (prevents JavaScript access), SameSite=Lax
  • Domain: .chika.page

4. Why We Use This Cookie

  • Rate Limiting: Track 20 queries per day limit
  • Chat History: Restore conversation on page reload (F5)
  • Session Persistence: Remember you across browser restarts

5. Third-Party Cookies

We do NOT use:

  • ❌ Google Analytics cookies
  • ❌ Facebook/Meta Pixel
  • ❌ Advertising cookies
  • ❌ Tracking cookies

6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies via your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Note: Blocking our cookie will prevent CHIKA from working (rate limiting won't function).

7. Cookie Consent

By using CHIKA, you consent to our use of this essential cookie. It's required for the service to function.

8. GDPR Compliance

Our cookie is strictly necessary for service delivery (GDPR Article 6(1)(b)). No consent banner required.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we add cookies in the future, we'll update this page and notify you.
